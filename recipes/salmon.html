Lemon Brown Butter Salmon

Description

A deliciously seasoned salmon that's easy to make on those busy weeknights after work.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tbsp. neutral oil
  • (6-oz) skin-on-salmon fillets
  • Kosher salt
  • 1 lemon
  • 4 Tbsp. unsalted butter, cubed
  • 1 large clove garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 Tbsp. chopped fresh parsley or dill

Steps

  1. In a large stainless steel skillet over medium heat, heat oil until shimmering. Pat salmon dry; season both sides with a heavy pinch of salt.
  2. Place salmon in skillet skin side down and cook, undisturbed, 4 minutes. Flip salmon, then finely grate lemon zest around salmon. Dot butter pieces around salmon, swirling pan to help melt butter and distribute zest. Cook 2 minutes.
  3. Cut zested lemon in half. Add garlic and juice from 1/2 lemon around salmon and cook, undisturbed, until salmon easily flakes with a fork and garlic is fragrant and slightly softened, about 1 minute more.
  4. Transfer salmon to a platter. Top with parsley.
