High Protein Chipotle Beef Potato Bowls

Description

A beef potato bowl that's packed with protein and simple to meal prep for the week.

Ingredients

Chipotle Beef

  • 1000g 95% Raw Ground/Minced Beef
  • 2 Tsp Salt
  • 2.5 Tsp Garlic Powder
  • 2.5 Tsp Onion Powder
  • 2.5 Tsp Paprika
  • 2 Tsp Parsley
  • 2 Tsp Cumin
  • 80g Chipotle Paste
  • 20g Honey
  • 20g Reduced Fat Butter (for cooking)
  • Splash of Water (if too dry)

Crispy Potatoes

  • 1200g Uncooked Potatoes, cubed
  • 2 Tsp Garlic Powder
  • 2 Tsp Onion Powder
  • 1.5 Tsp Chipotle Chilli Flakes (Omit for less heat)
  • 2 Tsp Parsley
  • 2 Tsp Smoked Paprika
  • 2 Tsp Cumin
  • Salt To Taste (after cooking)
  • 3 Tsp Olive Oil

Chipotle Sauce

  • 250g Low Fat Greek Yogurt (or yogurt of choice)
  • 100g Light Mayo
  • 80g Chipotle Paste
  • 25g Honey
  • Juice of 1/2 Lime
  • 1/2 Tsp each: Salt, Garlic, Paprika

Pico De Gallo

  • 300g Fresh Tomatoes, chopped
  • 150g Red Onion, chopped
  • 100g Red Bell Pepper, chopped
  • 100g Green Bell Pepper, chopped
  • 1.5 Tbsp Fresh Coriander, finely chopped
  • 1/2 Tsp each: Salt, Black Pepper
  • Juice of 1/2 Lime

Steps

    Crispy Potatoes
  1. Cut potatoes into small cubes.
  2. Add all seasoning and mix well with hands.
  3. Bake potatoes in oven or air fry for 20-23 minutes at 200C/400F until golden an crispy.
    4. Chipotle Sauce
  4. Mix all ingredients for Chipotle Sauce in a blender and blend until smooth. Bottle the sauce to save for the week.
    5. Chipotle Beef
  5. Add butter to pan and cook the beef on high heat for 6-8 minutes until fully brown.
  6. Add seasonings and chipotle paste, cook for 2-3 more minutes until golden.
  7. Add splash of water if too dry, lower the heat and add drizzle of honey. Mix.
    8. Pico De Gallo
  8. Add all ingredients for Pico De Gallo. Mix and store in a separate container to use throughout the week.
