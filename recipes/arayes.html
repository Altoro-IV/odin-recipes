Crispy Beef Arayes

High Protein Crispy Beef Arayes

Description

Easy to make beef arayes that are delicious and high in protein.

Ingredients

Arayes

  • 800g lean ground beef (5% or less)
  • 1 medium finely diced onion
  • 4 garlic cloves (minced or finely diced)
  • Fresh chopped coriander/cilantro
  • 2 finely chopped green chillies (optional if you like the heat)
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp back pepper
  • 1 tsp cayenne
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 5 sliced pittas (63g)
  • Cooking spray

Garlic White Sauce

  • 120g 0% Greek yoghurt
  • 60g light mayo
  • juice of half a lemon
  • 1-2 tsp honey
  • 1-2 tsp garlic powder

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients for arayes in a bowl and mix with your hands.
  2. Add 1/5 of the meat into each pitta bread.
  3. Spray pan with cooking spray
  4. Cook pittas on pan for 2-3 minutes each side until the outside is crispy and the meat is cooked through.
  5. Mix all ingredients for the Garlic White Sauce in a small bowl
